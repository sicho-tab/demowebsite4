Who We Are

About Evergreen College

Founded in 1989, Evergreen College has been a beacon of academic excellence in the region. Nestled in a serene green campus, we offer a nurturing environment where students grow intellectually, socially, and professionally.

Our institution is affiliated with the State University and is accredited with the highest grade. We offer undergraduate, postgraduate, and professional diploma programmes across Science, Arts, Commerce, and Technology.

With a legacy of producing industry leaders, government officers, scientists, and entrepreneurs, Evergreen College continues to shape futures with integrity and purpose.
